# 03 - SERVICE BLUEPRINT & CUSTOMER JOURNEY MAPPING

## Service Blueprint Architecture

A service blueprint visualizes all touchpoints a customer experiences with your brand, identifying moments of truth and optimization opportunities.

---

## Journey Stages

### Stage 1: Awareness
**Customer need:** Discover you exist
**Touchpoints:**
- Paid ads (Facebook, Google, LinkedIn)
- Organic search (SEO)
- Social media (Instagram, TikTok)
- Referral/word-of-mouth
- PR mentions

**Success metric:** Click-through rate (CTR) to website

**Optimization:**
- Ad copy matches intent
- Landing page matches ad promise
- Clear value proposition visible
- CTA prominent and compelling

---

### Stage 2: Consideration
**Customer need:** Evaluate if you solve their problem
**Touchpoints:**
- Landing page experience
- Competitive comparison
- Reviews/testimonials reading
- FAQ section exploration
- Contact form or discovery call

**Success metric:** Click-to-form rate, time on page, scroll depth

**Optimization:**
- Objection handling (FAQ, testimonials, guarantees)
- Competitive differentiation clear
- Social proof prominent
- Clear call-to-action for next step

---

### Stage 3: Decision
**Customer need:** Feel confident in their purchase
**Touchpoints:**
- Checkout experience
- Payment security signals
- Return policy clarity
- Customer support accessibility
- Final confirmation

**Success metric:** Conversion rate, checkout abandonment rate

**Optimization:**
- Minimize form fields
- Show security badges
- Clear return/guarantee policy
- Trust signals (testimonials, credentials)
- Single-click checkout if possible

---

### Stage 4: Retention
**Customer need:** Feel they made the right choice
**Touchpoints:**
- Welcome email sequence
- Onboarding experience
- Customer support accessibility
- Product delivery/access
- Success checkpoints

**Success metric:** Customer satisfaction (NPS), product usage

**Optimization:**
- Proactive support (email, chat)
- Clear next steps documented
- Progress tracking visible
- Quick wins to build momentum

---

### Stage 5: Expansion
**Customer need:** Additional value or premium tier
**Touchpoints:**
- Email nurture sequence
- Upsell recommendations
- Community/events
- Premium tier introduction
- Referral program

**Success metric:** Customer lifetime value (CLV), upsell rate

**Optimization:**
- Relevant recommendations (based on purchase)
- Exclusive benefits emphasized
- Social proof from existing premium customers
- Easy upgrade path

---

### Stage 6: Advocacy
**Customer need:** Share their success with others
**Touchpoints:**
- Referral program
- Testimonial request
- Social sharing
- Case study opportunity
- Community events

**Success metric:** Referral rate, organic word-of-mouth

**Optimization:**
- Easy referral mechanism (link, code)
- Incentive for both parties
- Case study visibility
- Community building

---

## Hidden Moments (Service Details)

### Micro-Moments to Optimize

**Sign-up Process**
- Current: "Create account" (generic)
- Optimized: "Join 2,400+ students, claim your spot" (specific, social proof)

**Confirmation Page**
- Current: "Thank you for signing up"
- Optimized: "You're in! Your first lesson drops Friday. Check your email for access."

**Welcome Email**
- Current: "Welcome to [service]"
- Optimized: "Meet your coach [Name]. Here's your personalized training plan for this week."

**First Interaction**
- Current: Generic onboarding email
- Optimized: Personalized message from founder/coach with specific next steps

**Support Contact**
- Current: General contact form
- Optimized: Live chat widget + instant response option + guaranteed response time

**Progress Indicator**
- Current: No progress visible
- Optimized: Dashboard showing lessons completed, skills improved, next milestone

## Customer Journey by Persona

### Aspirant (Identity Transformation)
1. **Awareness:** Testimonial mentioning transformation
2. **Consideration:** Before/after photos, student success stories
3. **Decision:** Guarantee removes risk ("If you don't improve in 30 days, full refund")
4. **Retention:** Progress tracking, milestone celebrations
5. **Expansion:** Premium coaching tier, community events
6. **Advocacy:** Share transformation, become case study

---

### Maximizer (Marginal Gains)
1. **Awareness:** Data-driven ad showing statistical improvements
2. **Consideration:** Detailed curriculum showing advanced techniques
3. **Decision:** Guarantees specific metrics ("Serve speed +5 mph in 8 weeks")
4. **Retention:** Weekly performance metrics, progress tracking
5. **Expansion:** Advanced techniques, personalized coaching
6. **Advocacy:** Share metrics improvement, recruit high-performers

---

### Pragmatist (Problem-Solving)
1. **Awareness:** Problem-focused ad ("Fix your serve timing in 3 sessions")
2. **Consideration:** Step-by-step solution visible
3. **Decision:** Clear implementation steps, quick results
4. **Retention:** Task checklist, results verification
5. **Expansion:** Next problem solved (footwork, consistency)
6. **Advocacy:** Recommend to friends with similar problem

---

### Community Seeker (Belonging)
1. **Awareness:** Community-focused ad ("Join 2,400+ tennis players")
2. **Consideration:** Community photos, member introductions
3. **Decision:** Access to community events, group activities
4. **Retention:** Regular community events, member spotlight
5. **Expansion:** Leadership opportunities, VIP community tier
6. **Advocacy:** Bring friends into community

---

### Status Striver (Premium Perception)
1. **Awareness:** Premium positioning ad (USTA pros, elite training)
2. **Consideration:** Credentials and exclusivity emphasized
3. **Decision:** Premium tier highlighted, scarcity of spots
4. **Retention:** VIP treatment, exclusive events
5. **Expansion:** Higher tier or mastermind group
6. **Advocacy:** Share elite status, recruit peers

---

### Skeptic (Evidence)
1. **Awareness:** Data-driven ad with specific metrics
2. **Consideration:** Third-party credentials, guarantees, research citations
3. **Decision:** Money-back guarantee, transparent methodology
4. **Retention:** Weekly data reports, transparent metrics
5. **Expansion:** Advanced analytics, personalized data analysis
6. **Advocacy:** Share data proof with others

## Content Requirements Per Stage

### Awareness
- Landing pages (1 per ad variant)
- Email sequences (3-email welcome)
- Social content (carousel ads, testimonials)
- Video content (product demo, founder story)

### Consideration
- Detailed curriculum/service description
- FAQ section (address 5-10 common objections)
- Testimonials/case studies
- Competitive comparison
- Risk reversal/guarantee clarity

### Decision
- Checkout experience optimized
- Security badges, trust signals
- Return policy clearly stated
- Customer support visible
- Terms & conditions clear

### Retention
- Onboarding email sequence (5-7 emails, 2 weeks)
- Help documentation (getting started, FAQ)
- Support response time guaranteed
- Success checkpoints defined

### Expansion
- Email nurture sequence (1 email every 3-4 days)
- Upsell recommendations
- Premium tier comparison
- Exclusive benefits for premium

### Advocacy
- Referral program mechanics
- Testimonial/case study request template
- Social sharing assets
- Community events promotion

## Implementation Roadmap

### Week 1: Map Current State
- Document all current touchpoints
- Identify gaps and friction points
- Measure baseline metrics (CTR, conversion, CAC)

### Week 2: Design Optimizations
- Create optimized experience per stage
- Design micro-moments improvements
- Plan persona-specific journeys

### Week 3: Implement Quick Wins
- Welcome email sequence
- Checkout form optimization
- FAQ section expansion
- CTA improvements

### Week 4: Launch & Measure
- Measure impact (conversion lift, engagement)
- A/B test variations
- Implement based on winners
