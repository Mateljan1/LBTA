Morning light on the court. The sound of the ball off a clean strike. Ocean breeze ten minutes from the water. The Pod is private player management built around a small, fixed group of serious juniors who train together, every day, with the same coach.

The structure.

Configuration

Two configurations. A 3-player Pod has two paying families plus Henry. A 4-player Pod has three. The number is fixed; there is no twentieth student waiting for a slot to open. The annual commitment is what makes the year-long planning possible — periodization, tournament selection, travel coaching, and the calls we take when something needs to be talked through outside a session.

One tuition covers everything: on-court training three to five days per week, individual development plans, tournament programming, travel coaching when families travel, and the standing availability you'd expect from a coach who knows your child by name. It is a year-long program commitment, not a per-session fee.
