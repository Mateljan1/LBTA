# Conversation Starters — LBTA Founder GPT (Andrew)

> Habit triggers, not curiosity prompts. Each starter maps to a real weekly task that takes Andrew 1–3 hours when done from scratch.
>
> Paste these (max 4) into the Conversation Starters field of the Custom GPT Builder.

---

## The 4 starters (paste these)

1. **Triage tonight's `support@` inbox and draft replies for everything I can send tomorrow morning.**
2. **Help me decide between 2–3 options on a tough call — give me the trade-offs and your pick with a confidence label.**
3. **Draft a parent or partner email from this thread or these notes — founder voice, 5 sentences, end with the next step.**
4. **Look at this Stripe / PlayByPoint / ActiveCampaign CSV and tell me the one thing I should look at first.**

---

## Why these four

- **#1 Inbox triage** — the single highest-volume time sink for a founder. This is what makes the GPT pay for itself in week one.
- **#2 Decision support** — turns the GPT into a chief-of-staff who pushes back on weak reasoning.
- **#3 Email drafting** — the most common "Andrew, can you reply to…" task, now answerable in under 60 seconds.
- **#4 Data triage** — when the data is messy and Andrew doesn't have time to read 200 rows, the GPT finds the headline.

---

## Backup starters (rotate seasonally)

- *"Plan my coaching week — what should be on court Tuesday-Saturday given current rosters?"*
- *"Prep me for my call with [parent / partner / coach] — what should I know, what should I ask?"*
- *"Audit one program for me — is the price, schedule, copy, and FAQ all consistent across the site?"*
- *"Write a 200-word weekly note to families — make it sound like me on a Sunday night."*

Swap in when seasonal needs change (e.g. summer camp prep, year-end retros, hiring sprints).

---

## Voice check rule (for Andrew's GPT)

Before any external draft, the GPT runs through:
- Forbidden words (none of: *elite, world-class, mastery, maximize, boost, unlock, level up, game-changer, transformational, unleash, dominate, crush it, sign up now, don't miss out, limited spots*)
- No exclamation points in subject lines
- No fake urgency or scarcity
- Ends with the next step, not hype

If any check fails, the GPT rewrites before showing the draft.

---

## When to swap a starter

If a starter isn't getting used after 2 weeks, swap it for one Andrew is actually copy-pasting into the GPT every week. The starter slot is precious — make every one of the four a habit.
