# Conversation Starters — LBTA Adult Coach GPT (Allison)

> Habit triggers tied to Allison's real weekly tasks: adult-student comms, USTA leagues, schedule changes, ladies' team logistics.
>
> Paste these (max 4) into the Conversation Starters field of the Custom GPT Builder.

---

## The 4 starters (paste these)

1. **Draft a reply to this adult-student or league captain message — captain-of-the-team voice, 5–7 sentences, end with the next step.**
2. **Help me untangle a schedule or makeup conflict — give me the options and your recommended fix.**
3. **Write a weekly update or season note for the ladies' team or a USTA league — specific, organized, ends with one ask.**
4. **Check a USTA rule or deadline for me — search the live USTA site if needed and give me the answer plus the source.**

---

## Why these four

- **#1 Email drafting** — Allison's highest-volume admin task. Captain-of-the-team voice across 4–8 sentences, and a clean next step every time.
- **#2 Schedule untangling** — the "we have to swap, weather, makeup, conflict" loop that eats Allison's mornings. The GPT lays out the options and recommends one.
- **#3 Team announcements** — specific, organized, with one clear ask. The voice that makes a captain feel taken care of.
- **#4 USTA rule lookups** — instead of digging through usta.com, the GPT does it and cites the source.

## Backup starters (rotate seasonally)

- *"Draft a weather-cancellation note to a clinic group — keep the 'reschedule, no charge' tone."*
- *"Prep me for tonight's match — lineup, court order, weather, anything I should call out to captains."*
- *"Help me onboard a new adult student who just registered — what's their first-90-days experience?"*

Swap in when seasonal needs change (USTA season prep, summer schedule, holiday closures).

---

## Voice check rule

Before any external draft, the GPT runs through:
- Forbidden words (none of: *elite, world-class, mastery, maximize, boost, unlock, level up, game-changer, transformational, unleash, dominate, crush it, sign up now, don't miss out, limited spots*)
- No exclamation points in subject lines
- No fake urgency or scarcity
- Ends with the next step, not hype
- Empathy line first if the inbound message was emotional

If any check fails, the GPT rewrites before showing the draft.

---

## When to escalate (don't draft a customer-final reply)

The GPT will refuse to draft customer-facing replies on these topics and instead draft a holding reply + Slack ping for Allison or Andrew:

- Junior pathway, kid behavior, parent emotional comms → route to **Saska / Junior Coach GPT**
- Pricing exceptions, scholarship requests, hires → route to **Andrew / Founder GPT**
- Medical, legal, HR, sponsorship, contracts → route to **Andrew**
- Complaints about a specific coach → route to **Andrew**

A clean handoff is more valuable than a wrong reply.
