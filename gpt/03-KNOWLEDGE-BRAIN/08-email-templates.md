## Drafting rules (for the GPT)

1. **Never send.** All Gmail actions are draft-only. Andrew or staff approves before send.
2. **Always pull the customer's name** from the email thread. Never use "Hi there" or "Hi friend" — it reads as a bot.
3. **Always include exactly one CTA.** A trial link, a call number, or a reply prompt. Not all three.
4. **Never use the personal "Andrew" line** unless the email is going out as Andrew personally (HP inquiry, founder follow-up, scholarship reply). Default to support@.
5. **Never invent prices, dates, or coach assignments.** If something isn't in this knowledge file, draft a placeholder and flag for human review.
6. **Run the voice check (file 05) before saving the draft.**
