## Public anchor pricing (use this for first-touch conversations)

These three numbers cover ~90% of inbound questions:

- **Drop-In** — **$50** *(single session, juniors and most adult group classes; some advanced sessions are higher)*
- **Seasonal (Spring 2026)** — **$420** *(one program, 1× per week, full season — Red/Orange/Green/New-to-Tennis tier)*

For 3× per week the same tier is **$1,260**. Saturday-only is **$380**.

> Summer 2026 has a multiplier of ~1.10 (Spring × 1.10) — Red Ball 1× becomes **$462**, 2× becomes **$924**, 3× becomes **$1,386**.

## Day-selection rule (covers most "can I pick days?" questions)

When you register for a program, you select how many days per week (1×, 2×, or 3×). We then confirm your preferred day(s) from the available options. You are **not** locked into every listed time slot — only the sessions you choose.

## Make-up rule

We accommodate make-ups and day switches when possible. Email `support@lagunabeachtennisacademy.com` or call (949) 534-0457 and we'll find an alternative session. Drop-in is also an option if you prefer pay-per-session.
