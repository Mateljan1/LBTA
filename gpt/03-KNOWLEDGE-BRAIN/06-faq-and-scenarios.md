## Bookings & trials

**1. How do I book a trial?**
> Easiest path: `lagunabeachtennisacademy.com/book` — pick the program, pick the day, you're set. If you'd rather chat first, call (949) 534-0457 or email support@lagunabeachtennisacademy.com.

**2. Is there a fee for the trial?**
> Most trials are free. A few specialty sessions (High Performance, certain camps) have a discounted trial rate — confirm at /book or I can check for you. We back the trial with our 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee.

**3. How quickly will someone get back to me?**
> During the day, within a couple of hours. After hours, by the next morning. If you need same-day help, call (949) 534-0457.

**4. Can my kid try a class without committing?**
> Yes. Book a trial at `/book`, show up, and decide after. We don't push registration until the trial is done and you're sure.

## Pricing

**5. How much is a single class?**
> Drop-in is **$50** for most junior and adult group classes. Junior Development and High Performance drop-in is $70–$100. Single private lessons start at **$120 with Peter or Allison**, **$250 with Andrew**.

**6. What's the cheapest way to start?**
> A drop-in at $50 is the lowest-commitment way. If you know you'll come for the full season, the **Seasonal package at $420** is the better value (1× per week, full season). The Committed package at $840 (2× per week) is the best per-session rate.

**7. Do you offer payment plans?**
> Yes. Email `support@lagunabeachtennisacademy.com` and we'll set up a two-payment or three-payment plan. We don't charge interest.

**8. Do you offer scholarships?**
> Yes. Need-based scholarships are available, decided by Andrew personally. Apply at `lagunabeachtennisacademy.com/apply-scholarship`. We typically respond within a week.

**9. Sibling discount?**
> 10% off for additional siblings in the same program. Add both at registration and the discount applies automatically.

## Programs & placement

**10. My child is 7 and brand new — where do they start?**
> Orange Ball at Moulton Meadows. 60-minute group session, ages 7–8, $420 for one day a week or $840 for two. Coach Peter runs most of these.

**11. My child is 12, already plays, what UTR are they?**
> If you don't know yet, book a Junior Development trial — we'll evaluate and tell you straight. UTR 1–4 goes Tier 1, UTR 4–7 goes Tier 2, UTR 8+ moves to High Performance.

**12. I'm an adult, never played, where do I start?**
> New to Tennis at LBHS with Coach Allison. 1-hour session, **$420 for the season** (1× per week) or **$615 for 2× per week**. No rating needed, no equipment needed — bring sneakers.

**13. I played in college, where do I fit?**
> Adult Advanced (NTRP 4.0+) at LBHS. 2-hour session, $625 for the season. Andrew or Allison teach this. Or USTA league play if you want match-play volume.

**14. I want to play in matches, not just train.**
> Two paths: **UTR Match Play** every Saturday and Sunday at Alta Laguna ($50 drop-in, season pass available), or **USTA Adult Leagues** ($393–$458 per league season + $48 USTA membership). Allison runs the league pathway.

## Camps

**15. When is summer camp?**
> Tennis & Adventure runs Mon–Thu, June 15 through August 29. Ages 5–11 at Alta Laguna Park. **$495 full day, $325 half day**. Junior Training Camp (ages 12–17) is at LBHS, **$595 full day, $325 half day**.

**16. Can I book one day instead of a full week?**
> Yes. Drop-in is $99/day full day, $85/day half day for the 5–11 camp. Email or call to book individual days.

**17. What if it rains?**
> We swap to an available alternate day in the same week first. If no swap is possible, you get make-up credit good for any future camp or program. Pro-rated refunds available on request.

**18. Do I need to register for every week or one time?**
> Each week is its own registration. Book the weeks you want at `/camps`. Multi-week discount: 10% off when you book 4+ weeks together.

## Coaches

**19. Who teaches my child?**
> Depends on the program. Peter runs most juniors and all camps. Allison runs adults and women's leagues. Andrew runs High Performance and ATP-level privates. I can recommend a fit if you tell me your child's age and level.

**20. Can I request a specific coach?**
> For private lessons, yes — pick the coach when you book. For group programs, the coach is set by program. If a coach isn't a fit after the first session, we'll find a different option.

**21. Is Robert still coaching?**
> Robert is no longer on the LBTA team. Our current coaches are **Andrew Mateljan**, **Peter DeFrantz**, and **Allison Cronk**. Happy to introduce you to whichever fits best.

## Locations

**22. Where do you train?**
> Three locations: **Moulton Meadows Park** (1098 Balboa Ave) for most juniors, **Alta Laguna Park** for all camps and UTR Match Play, and **Laguna Beach High School (LBHS)** for adult programs and High Performance.

**23. Is there parking?**
> Yes at all three. Moulton Meadows has a public lot. Alta Laguna has on-street and lot parking. LBHS has school lot parking after 3pm and on weekends.

## Policies

**24. What's your cancellation policy?**
> We ask for 48 hours on group sessions and 24 hours on private lessons, but we're flexible. Day swaps within the same week are routine — just email or call.

**25. What if my kid hates it?**
> 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee. Within 30 days, we refund unused tuition in full. After day 30, remaining sessions convert to account credit you can use anytime. Email support@lagunabeachtennisacademy.com and we make it right.

**26. Do you allow make-up sessions?**
> Yes. Tell us in advance and we'll find an available alternate session in the same week. We're flexible — we'd rather you train than skip.

**27. Do parents stay on the court?**
> Drop-off model. Coaching happens between coach and player. You're welcome to watch from the sideline. Concerns get routed to the coach by email after the session.

## Edge cases

**28. Can you accommodate a child with [allergy / medical condition / IEP]?**
> Yes, in most cases. Tell us at registration so the coach can plan. For severe allergies, we keep medical info on hand at every session. Email support@lagunabeachtennisacademy.com to discuss specifics.

**29. Do you do birthday parties or private group events?**
> On request. Email support@lagunabeachtennisacademy.com with the date, group size, and age range, and we'll quote it.

**30. Can I get a refund for sessions I didn't use?**
> Within 30 days of your start date, yes — full refund for unused sessions. After 30 days, remaining sessions convert to account credit usable anytime. Special circumstances? Email us — we settle on the side of the player.
