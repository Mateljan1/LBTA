# 04 — Policies (refunds, weather, cancellation, conduct)

> The homepage promise is canonical. The 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee wins. The FAQ has been aligned to it (April 2026). When in doubt, send the customer to `support@lagunabeachtennisacademy.com` or (949) 534-0457 — we settle on the side of the player.

## Refund policy — 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Every program is backed by a **30-Day Money-Back Guarantee**.

- **Within the first 30 days of training:** If LBTA isn't the right fit, we refund unused tuition in full. No questions, no friction.
- **After day 30:** Remaining sessions convert to **LBTA account credit** that can be applied to any future program, camp, or private lesson. Account credit does not expire while you remain a customer.

> The reason this works: if a family isn't a fit, we'd rather find out early than have them grind through a season. Trial bookings exist to surface that *before* a 30-day clock starts.

## Cancellation — group programs

- We ask for **48 hours notice** to cancel or move a group session, but we are flexible. Day swaps within the same week are routine — email or call.
- If a session is missed and not rescheduled in advance, we'll find a make-up slot when possible (drop-in availability, same program, different day).

## Cancellation — private lessons

- We ask for **24 hours notice** to cancel or reschedule a private lesson.
- Less than 24 hours: the lesson may still be charged unless rescheduled within 7 days at the coach's discretion.
- Same-day cancellations due to illness or weather: not charged.

## Weather

- **Rain or unsafe conditions:** Sessions are rescheduled at no charge. We notify families via email and SMS as early as possible.
- **Camp weather day:** First, we attempt to swap to an available alternate day in the same week. If no swap is possible, the day converts to **make-up credit** good for any future camp week or program. Pro-rated refunds available on request.
- **Heat:** When temperatures exceed safe-play thresholds at the published court, sessions move indoors when available, are shortened, or rescheduled.

## Player and parent conduct

LBTA is a coaching environment, not a country club. We expect:

- **Players:** show up on time, with water, racquet, and proper court shoes. Phones off-court. Respect the coach, the court, and the other players.
- **Parents:** drop-off model. Coaching happens between coach and player. Concerns are routed to the coach by email — not on-court mid-session.
- **Coaches:** plan every session, treat every player as an athlete in development, and surface issues to parents within 48 hours.

## Waiver and media release (summary)

> We collect a Player Participation Waiver and a Photo/Video Media Release at the time of registration. The official text lives in our PlayByPoint registration system; this section is a plain-language summary so the GPT can answer "what am I signing?"

**Player Participation Waiver — what it covers:**

1. Acknowledgment that tennis is a physical activity with inherent risk of injury.
2. Confirmation that the player is medically fit to participate; LBTA is not responsible for unreported pre-existing conditions.
3. Authorization for emergency medical care if a guardian cannot be reached.
4. Agreement to follow LBTA conduct expectations (above).

**Photo/Video Media Release — what it covers:**

1. Permission for LBTA to use photos and video of the player in marketing materials (website, social, email).
2. Players are never identified by full name in public marketing without explicit additional consent.
3. Opt-out is always available — email `support@lagunabeachtennisacademy.com` and we remove the player from the media library.

> **GPT behavior:** If a parent asks "what does the waiver say," summarize the four points above. If they ask for the verbatim text, route to PlayByPoint registration and offer to email a copy.

## Privacy and data

- We do not sell or share customer data.
- Newsletter and updates: opt-in at registration; opt-out at any time.
- Data is stored in our CRM (ActiveCampaign) and registration system (PlayByPoint).

## Scholarship policy

LBTA offers a need-based scholarship program. Applications at `/apply-scholarship`. Decisions are made by Andrew Mateljan personally. Scholarship coverage ranges from 25% to 100% of tuition based on family circumstance. We do not publicly disclose scholarship recipients.

## Insurance

LBTA carries general liability insurance. Certificates of insurance available on request for parks, schools, and venue partners.

## What we never do

- We do not pressure-sell. The trial reveals fit; the family decides.
- We do not run lock-in contracts longer than one season.
- We do not lock account credit to a sunset date while the customer is active.
- We do not poach players from other coaches in Laguna Beach. The community is small.
