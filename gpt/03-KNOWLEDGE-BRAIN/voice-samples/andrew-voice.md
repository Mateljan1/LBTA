## Topic coverage suggestions (pick a range)

To get a representative voice profile, aim for at least one example of each:

- ✅ Short logistics reply (2–4 sentences).
- ✅ Longer thoughtful reply (parent asking about pathway, philosophy).
- ✅ Gentle pushback (saying no without making them feel wrong).
- ✅ Pricing or refund question.
- ✅ "Tell me more about your academy" prospect inquiry.
- ✅ Warm thank-you / acknowledgment.
- ✅ Missed-class / weather / make-up logistics.
- ✅ Coach assignment / placement question.
- ✅ Decision delegation ("here's what I'd think about").
- ✅ Public marketing-adjacent reply (newsletter response, social DM).
- ✅ A "no" — politely declining a request that doesn't fit.
- ✅ A boundary — protecting another member's privacy or another coach's time.

## Anti-samples (do NOT include)

- Emails about a specific player's medical, custodial, or safety situation.
- Emails written in frustration or late at night that don't represent voice in steady state.
- Emails that are 80%+ a canned template.
- Emails where the parent's identity is partially recoverable even after anonymization.
