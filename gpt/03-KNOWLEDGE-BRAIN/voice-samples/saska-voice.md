## Topic coverage for Saska

Aim for at least one example of each:

- ✅ Weekly group recap to a parent distribution (1-line group, 2 specifics, 1 at-home tip).
- ✅ Parent asks "how is my kid doing?" — concrete progress signal, no future-pacing.
- ✅ Camp packing list / drop-off / pickup logistics.
- ✅ Kid is hesitant or new to the group — confidence-building parent reply.
- ✅ Parent asks about moving up to next group level.
- ✅ Parent asks about adding private lessons.
- ✅ Holiday or school-break schedule change.
- ✅ Camp registration confirmation, warm + specific.
- ✅ Coaching observation a parent should know (positive, factual — NOT "she's headed for D1").
- ✅ A parent gently corrected (e.g. "thanks for the suggestion — let's give the cadence two more weeks before we adjust").
- ✅ Welcome to a new family.
- ✅ Acknowledgment: parent shared something the kid said about practice.

## Junior comms — anti-samples (do NOT include)

- ❌ Anything about a kid's medical, mental-health, behavioral, or safety situation.
- ❌ Anything about a custody/separation situation.
- ❌ Any email where another kid in the group is named, even with first name only — privacy of OTHER kids matters too.
- ❌ Frustration drafts or venting drafts.
- ❌ Predictive language about a kid's future ("she's going to be amazing," "he's destined for college tennis"). The Junior Coach GPT must NOT generate this — and our voice samples should not either.
