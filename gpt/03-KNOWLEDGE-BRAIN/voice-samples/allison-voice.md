## Topic coverage for Allison

Aim for at least one example of each:

- ✅ Weekly clinic recap (3–5 sentences, what we worked on, one at-home tip).
- ✅ League team comms (USTA / UTR / 9.0–10.0+ logistics).
- ✅ Member missed a clinic — calm follow-up, no shaming.
- ✅ Member asks a tactical question (specific shot, footwork pattern).
- ✅ Member asks "where do I go from here?" — pathway/level question.
- ✅ Doubles partner request / partner conflict.
- ✅ Member asks about competing in a tournament.
- ✅ Welcome back / off-season check-in.
- ✅ Gentle nudge after several missed sessions.
- ✅ Recommending a private lesson without being pushy.
- ✅ Practice partner introductions across two members.
- ✅ Boundary: declining a request gracefully (e.g. "can you write a USTA recommendation for me?" when it's not appropriate).

## Anti-samples (do NOT include)

- Emails involving a member's medical, mental-health, or relationship situation.
- Frustration emails / venting drafts.
- Emails written 100% as canned templates.
- Anything that identifies a specific member after anonymization.
