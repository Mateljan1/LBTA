# Parent Consent Language — Optional Add-On to Enrollment

> Plain-language consent that members can sign as part of enrollment IF we want explicit acknowledgment that AI may draft routine communications. Counsel-readable; not a wall of legalese. Optional — not required to enroll.

---

## When to use this

We do not require this consent today. It's here for if/when:
- Counsel recommends explicit consent.
- A member asks for a written record of how we use AI.
- We expand AI usage in a way that touches the member directly (e.g. an AI chat assistant on our website).

## The language (≤ 200 words)

> **Use of AI in LBTA Communications**
>
> Laguna Beach Tennis Academy uses AI tools (specifically OpenAI ChatGPT, configured as Custom GPTs) to help draft routine communications faster — schedule confirmations, weekly group recaps, weather updates, and answers to frequently asked questions. Every message we send is read and approved by a coach before sending. We do not auto-send AI-drafted communications.
>
> AI is **not** used to handle:
> - Personal communications about your child's progress, behavior, or wellbeing.
> - Emotional, safety-related, custodial, or medical conversations.
> - Any communication you specifically request to be human-only.
>
> **AI does not have access to your payment information**, which is handled by Stripe (a separate vendor) on our website at `/book` and through `playbypoint.com`.
>
> By enrolling at LBTA, you acknowledge that some of the routine communications you receive from us may have started as AI-drafted text, reviewed and edited by a coach before send. You may opt out of this at any time by emailing `support@lagunabeachtennisacademy.com` or calling `(949) 534-0457`. Opting out has no effect on your enrollment, billing, or experience at the academy.
>
> ☐ I acknowledge LBTA's use of AI as described above.

## What this is NOT

- Not a full privacy policy. (See `privacy-disclosure.md`.)
- Not a clickwrap on the website (yet). Today: optional add-on to the enrollment paper.
- Not a release of liability for AI errors. We remain responsible for everything we send. AI doesn't shift that responsibility.

## What to do if a parent declines

- Honor it. Add a tag to their member file: **"Prefers human-only communications."**
- Coaches write all that family's emails by hand.
- No record-keeping beyond the tag.
- No follow-up. No friction.
