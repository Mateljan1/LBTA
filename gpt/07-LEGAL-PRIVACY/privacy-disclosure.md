# LBTA AI Use — Privacy Disclosure

> The plain-language statement of how LBTA uses AI in its communications and operations. Designed to be (1) readable by a parent in 90 seconds, (2) defensible if reviewed by counsel, (3) usable as the website footer / FAQ language.

---

## How we use AI at LBTA

Laguna Beach Tennis Academy uses **OpenAI ChatGPT (Custom GPTs)** to help us draft routine communications faster — schedule confirmations, weekly group recaps, weather updates, and answers to common scheduling and policy questions.

Every message we send is read and approved by a coach before it's sent. We don't auto-send AI-generated communications.

## What we use AI for

- Drafting routine logistics emails (schedules, weather, weekly recaps).
- Drafting answers to frequently asked questions about programs, prices, and policies.
- Drafting public-facing copy on our website and marketing materials.
- Internal note-taking and analysis (e.g. anonymized roster summaries).

## What we do NOT use AI for

- Sending any communication without a coach reviewing it first.
- Generating personal advice about your child's progress, performance, or wellbeing.
- Handling emotional, safety-related, custodial, or medical conversations.
- Storing your payment information, contact information, or family details in any AI system.
- Creating images of real players or impersonating any specific coach in first person to outside audiences.

## What information we share with AI

The Custom GPTs we use are loaded with **publicly available information only**: our website copy, published programs and prices, public schedules, and brand voice samples (with all member names, contact information, and identifying details removed before they're loaded).

We do **not** load:
- Member rosters, contact information, or family data.
- Payment or financial information.
- Coach personnel records.
- Any private communications with members.

## Where AI does and doesn't appear in your experience with LBTA

- **Routine email reply** (e.g. "When does the Tuesday clinic meet?") → likely AI-drafted, coach-reviewed before send.
- **Personal email about your child** → coach-written, end-to-end.
- **On-court coaching** → 100% human, always.
- **Phone calls** → 100% human, always.
- **The chat assistant on our website (if active)** → AI, with strict guardrails. Routes to a coach for anything specific.

## Your choice

If you'd prefer that your communications with us never include any AI-drafted content, just tell us and we'll honor it. Email `support@lagunabeachtennisacademy.com` or text `(949) 534-0457`. There's no penalty, no question asked, and no record beyond a note in your member file.

## Vendor and tool disclosure

The AI tools we use are:
- **OpenAI ChatGPT** (Custom GPTs and the underlying model).
- **Google Workspace** (Gmail and Drive — for drafting and document storage).

Neither of these tools has access to your payment information, which is handled by **Stripe** (a separate vendor) on our website at `/book` and through our court booking platform `playbypoint.com`.

## Data retention

ChatGPT conversations are subject to OpenAI's standard data retention policies. We do not store member-identifying information in ChatGPT. The voice samples used to train our drafting GPTs are anonymized before upload — names, contact details, and identifying information are removed.
