### 1. Voice samples

I authorize Andrew Mateljan to:
- Pull up to 12 of my past emails (member and parent communication) from the past 12 months.

### 2. Gmail integration

I authorize my LBTA Gmail account (`[coach]@lagunabeachtennisacademy.com`) to be connected to my Custom GPT with the following scopes:
- ✅ Read messages
- ✅ Create drafts
- ❌ Send mail (NOT granted)

I understand:
- The GPT can read my email threads when I ask it to.
- The GPT can save drafts to my Gmail Drafts folder.
- The GPT cannot send anything on my behalf.
- Conversations with the GPT may include the contents of my emails as context.
- I can disconnect Gmail at any time through ChatGPT → Configure → Connected Apps.

**I consent to this integration:** ☐ Yes ☐ No

### 3. Drive access (read-only, scoped to one folder)

I understand that the GPT will have read access only to the folder `LBTA / Public-Safe Assets/` in Andrew's Google Drive, and not to any other Drive content.

**I acknowledge:** ☐ Yes

## What this consent does NOT cover

- I am not consenting to my image, name, or identity being used in any AI-generated image.
- I am not consenting to the GPT speaking in first person as me to anyone outside our internal team.
- I am not consenting to the GPT being used by any other coach.
- I am not consenting to my email content being shared with any third party beyond OpenAI (the underlying ChatGPT vendor) and Google (Gmail/Drive vendors).

## My rights

- I can revoke this consent at any time by telling Andrew. He pauses my GPT within the day.
- I can ask for any voice sample to be removed at any time.
- I can ask to see what's in my GPT's knowledge files at any time.
- I can decline to use the GPT entirely — there is no employment consequence.
