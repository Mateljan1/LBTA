# LBTA Custom GPTs — Internal Terms of Use

> The internal rules everyone using an LBTA GPT agrees to follow. Plain language. Counter-signed by Andrew + the coach.

---

## Who this applies to

- Andrew Mateljan (Founder, owner of all 6 GPTs)
- Allison [Last Name] (Adult Coach GPT)
- Saska [Last Name] (Junior Coach GPT)
- Any future coach or staff member granted access to an LBTA GPT.

The Front Desk GPT, when public, is governed by the **Privacy Disclosure** in this folder, not by these internal terms.

## What you agree to

By using an LBTA Custom GPT, the user agrees:

### 1. Read every draft before you send

The GPT drafts. You ship. Every email, recap, post, or message that leaves your account has been read by you. **No exceptions for "routine" sends.**

### 2. Don't paste member PII into the GPT

When uploading a file or pasting an email thread for context:
- Replace last names with first name + initial.
- Replace phone numbers with `(XXX) XXX-XXXX`.
- Replace email addresses with `[email]`.
- For uploaded CSVs: anonymize first (see `04-INTEGRATIONS/code-interpreter-policy.md`).

If you're unsure whether something is safe to paste, ask Andrew. **Default to not pasting.**

### 3. Use the GPT in voice — not as voice

The GPT drafts a starting point. The voice on the page is yours. If a draft doesn't sound like you, edit until it does, or reject and start over.

If voice drift becomes a pattern (3+ off-voice drafts in a week), tell Andrew at the next Friday Compound Review. Don't keep shipping drafts you don't believe in.

### 4. Never claim the GPT wrote it

When asked by a member or parent whether AI was involved:
- If the email started as an AI draft and you edited and shipped it: *"I drafted with help from a tool, edited, and sent."*
- If you wrote it from scratch: *"I wrote it from scratch."*
- Never say "AI did it" — you reviewed, you shipped, you own it.

### 5. Don't use the GPT for anything outside LBTA work

The GPT is provisioned for LBTA work. Personal drafts, side projects, or non-LBTA writing should use a separate ChatGPT account or your own personal Custom GPT.

### 6. Report incidents same-day

If the GPT:
- Made up a fact a member or parent saw.
- Used a forbidden word in a sent email.
- Leaked any internal info.
- Did anything weird, off, or concerning.

→ Tell Andrew the same day. See `05-OPERATIONS/runbooks/`.

### 7. Don't share access to the GPT

The GPT is provisioned per-coach. **Don't share your ChatGPT login** with another coach, even for a quick draft. If another coach needs to use a GPT, they get their own access through Andrew.

### 8. The GPT does not replace coaching judgment

For decisions about a player, a parent conflict, a refund question, or a safety issue:
- The GPT can draft a holding reply.
- The GPT does not make the call.
- Route to Andrew for anything ambiguous.

### 9. Friday Compound Review attendance

Every Friday at 4pm. 30 minutes (or 15 for your slot). This is not optional during the first 90 days. After 90 days, attendance becomes monthly minimum.

### 10. Privacy of incoming communication

When the GPT reads a Gmail thread to draft a reply, the contents of that thread stay in your ChatGPT session. Don't take screenshots of GPT conversations that include member emails and share them outside the team. If you want to show Andrew a thread, do it in person or via a direct DM.

## What Andrew agrees to

- Build, maintain, and update the GPTs on a clear cadence (Friday weekly, end-of-month refresh, quarterly review).
- Take incidents seriously and fix root causes within 5 business days.
- Never share GPT-generated drafts about a coach with that coach's parents/members without coach approval.
- Treat coach feedback on voice and adoption as primary signal — coaches' instincts on their own voice override Andrew's.
- Pause or rebuild a GPT if a coach asks for it after a clear incident.

## Termination

Either party can pause GPT access at any time, no questions asked. The user simply tells Andrew, and Andrew flips sharing to "Only me" within the day. The GPT is a tool; opt-out is always available.

## Effective date

This agreement is effective on the date the coach activates their GPT (sees their first draft). It remains in effect until either party pauses access.
