# LBTA Canva Asset Brief — Photos & Videos We Need

**Use this list in Canva** to create or curate every photo and video for the site. Each row is one asset. Export from Canva at the sizes below; we’ll convert to WebP and add to the project. **No stock imagery** — real LBTA players, coaches, and courts only.

**Choosing from your existing library?** See **`plans/what-shots-work-best.md`** for what sells most, what to look for (pro, junior, adult, founder), and a “find this first” checklist — then match those picks to the filenames and folders here.

---

## Quick reference: formats

| Type | Export from Canva | We use on site |
|------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Hero / full-bleed** | 1920×1080 px (16:9) | WebP ≤350KB |
| **Card / section** | 800×600 px (4:3) or 800×800 (1:1) | WebP ≤200KB |
| **Portrait (coach/testimonial)** | 600×800 px (3:4) or 800×1000 (4:5) | WebP ≤250KB |
| **Video (hero)** | 1920×1080, 15–30 sec, MP4 | WebM + MP4, poster image |
| **Video (testimonial/short)** | 16:9, 30–90 sec | Host on Vimeo; we embed |
