LBTA UTR Match Play Series

Season 1, Week 1 of 8
starts tomorrow

Good matches. Good people. Your UTR moves after every match.

We’re excited to kick off Week 1 of the LBTA UTR Match Play Series.

This is an 8-week run of Saturday and Sunday match play from April 11 through May 30, with Grand Finals Night on June 6.

Players are paired by level, not age or gender. Formats can change week to week to keep competition sharp, fun, and development-focused.

Saturday, April 11 - Alta Laguna Park

Alta Laguna Park

3300 Alta Laguna Blvd, Laguna Beach, CA 92651

Color Ball Match Play (Red / Orange / Green) - 1:30-4:00 PM

Check-in: 1:00 PM

For kids who can rally with red, orange, or green balls and are ready to start playing real points, not just doing drills. Underhand serves and drop feeds are welcome, and we keep formats simple so new competitors feel successful.

Coaches are on court for every match to run the scoring, teach basic tactics, and keep the environment positive and fun. We announce matchups at the event so players get fresh opponents and lots of point play every week.

Why this series matters

This is verified match play that counts toward your UTR rating every week.

You earn season points for wins, upsets, attendance streaks, and special formats. Week 1 is double points, so this is the best week to jump in.

Grand Finals Night on June 6 features division championships, awards, an end-of-season party, and a Karue Sell exhibition. Division winners play doubles alongside Karue in front of the whole group.
