Coach’s Tip from Andrew Mateljan

“Big Target First.”

In neutral rallies, aim to the biggest target first — usually the middle or a deep crosscourt. Most points are lost, not won; one more safe ball often decides it.

— Andrew Mateljan, Founder & Academy Director
