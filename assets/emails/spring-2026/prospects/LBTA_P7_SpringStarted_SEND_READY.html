A FRAMEWORK FOR YOUR FIRST SESSION — EARN THE LINE

Here’s something useful for your first time on court, whether you’re a total beginner or coming back after years away.

The most common mistake recreational players make isn’t technical. It’s going down the line at the wrong moment. Down the line has a higher net, a shorter court, and half the margin of crosscourt. If your feet aren’t set and you’re not balanced, crosscourt is always the better choice.

The rule we teach: you have to earn the line. Short ball, feet set? Go for it. Deep ball, still moving? Crosscourt. Every time.

Try it: In your trial class, or your next match, make crosscourt your default until a short ball invites you to change direction. Notice how many fewer errors you make.
