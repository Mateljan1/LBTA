A FRAMEWORK FOR ADULTS — FIRST 4 SHOTS

Here’s the insight that changes how adults practice: roughly 70% of recreational points end by the fourth shot.

That means the 30-ball rally you’re worried about? It almost never happens. The serve, the return, and the next ball after each — that’s where matches are decided.

If you want to improve fast as an adult, don’t spend all your time on baseline rallying. Spend it on the first four shots. Know where you’re serving and what you’re doing next. Block the return deep and recover. Simple. Effective. Immediately applicable.

This is what we work on in every adult group — not just technique, but the decisions that actually win points.

Try it: Next time you play, plan your serve+1 before every service point. Just those two shots. Where is the serve going, and where is the next ball going? You’ll feel calmer and win more points.
