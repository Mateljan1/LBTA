How It Works

The LBTA App

The app is how we manage everything now. Download it and log in with your existing account — here's what to expect.

Haven't paid yet? You can still book classes through the app using the Pay Later option. Once we receive your receipt from the City of Laguna Beach, we'll load your credits
Credits loaded automatically — after your City payment is confirmed, we load your package credits into the app and book you into your classes
See your credits — you'll see your spring session credits and how many you have left to use this quarter
Cancel or reschedule — can't make it? Cancel and reschedule to another date directly in the app. Your credit goes back to your account (same for rain-outs)
No more texting to confirm — the app handles reminders and updates. No need to text us about attendance

Turn on push notifications

Enable notifications in the LBTA app so you get class reminders automatically. You'll also receive email confirmations — check your spam/junk folder if you're not seeing them.

This Weekend

UTR Match Play — Week 2

UTR (Universal Tennis Rating) is a globally recognized rating system that updates after every match. Unlike traditional ratings, it reflects exactly where you are right now — and it works for every level, from kids learning to rally to adults competing in leagues.

Every match in our series is submitted and verified through UTR. You finish the season with a real, portable rating.

Don't have a rating yet? That's exactly what this is for. Just tell us a little about your background and we'll place you in the right division. No experience needed — just show up ready to play.

Who can play

Open to all LBTA players — juniors and adults. Players are matched by level. Red Ball, Orange Ball, and Green Dot players compete on scaled courts with a coach present — it's the best way to introduce kids to real match play early on. Can't serve yet? No problem — drop-feed starts are fine. Development, High Performance, and adult players compete in UTR-rated singles and doubles. No minimum rating required.
