If you have a junior player (ages 10–18) who enjoys competition and thrives on belonging, there's something worth knowing about.

Junior Team Tennis (JTT) is our USTA-affiliated team program. Players train together and compete against other academies and clubs as a team.

It's a great fit for players who:

  • Want structured practices + weekend matches
  • Thrive with accountability and team culture
  • Are building confidence through match play
  • Enjoy camaraderie alongside competition

Our JTT values align with everything we do at LBTA: Movement. Discipline. Belonging.

This isn't required—and not every player needs it. But for the right athlete, it can be transformative.

LBTA Insight

Why Belonging Matters

Tennis is often called an individual sport—but the best development happens in community. Young kids gain confidence from being part of a group. Juniors push each other to improve. Adults find motivation in showing up for teammates. That's why "Belonging" is one of our three pillars.

If you'd like to learn more, reply to this email and we'll share the details.

P.S. If JTT isn't the right fit, just let us know what you're looking for—we're happy to point you in the right direction.
