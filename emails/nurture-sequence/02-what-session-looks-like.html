No matter the age or level, LBTA sessions follow a clear rhythm.

For younger players (ages 3–11):

  • Structured warm-ups that build coordination
  • Technical fundamentals through movement-based games
  • Confidence-building rallies and fun competition
  • Steady progression to bigger courts as skills develop

For competitive juniors (ages 11–17):

  • Focused technical and tactical instruction
  • Point-play scenarios and match preparation
  • Physical conditioning integrated into every session
  • UTR tracking and tournament readiness

For adults (all levels):

  • Clear instruction tailored to your current game
  • Drill-based improvement, not just hitting
  • Doubles tactics, singles strategy, or fitness-focused play
  • Flexible scheduling that fits real life

Every session ends with purpose—players leave knowing what they worked on and what's next.
