Why Team Tennis Works
(Especially for Newer Players)

Most kids at LBTA aren't tournament players. They're learning fundamentals in group classes, hitting with friends, maybe taking a private lesson here and there.

But without real matches, progress plateaus. They don't learn:

  • How to problem-solve when a pattern isn't working
  • How to stay composed when they're losing
  • How to execute under pressure instead of just rallying

Team tennis gives them match experience without the isolation. They're playing for teammates who understand the nerves. There's a coach present giving real-time feedback. And they're learning to compete in an environment that's challenging but not overwhelming.

Home matches are at Laguna Beach High School and Alta Laguna Park. We play teams from neighboring Orange County clubs. And unlike most junior programs, we have a coach at every match — not just practices.

Your child gets the reps. We make sure they're learning from them.
