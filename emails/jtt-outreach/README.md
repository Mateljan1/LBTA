# JTT Spring 2026 Outreach Campaign

Complete email campaign system for JTT recruitment.

---

## 📧 **3 Email Templates**

### **Email 1: General LBTA Families**
**File:** `01-general-lbta-families.html`  
**Subject:** "Spring Junior Team Tennis — Spots Now Available"  
**Use for:** Current/past LBTA students and contacts  
**Goal:** Announce JTT and drive registrations  

**Key Points:**
- JTT is USTA league competition
- 3× weekly practices + weekend matches
- Divisions for ages 10U-18U
- Coach support included

---

### **Email 2: LBHS Girls Tennis**
**File:** `02-lbhs-girls-tennis.html`  
**Subject:** "Quick note about off-season training"  
**Use for:** LBHS Girls Tennis Fall 2024 roster  
**Goal:** Personal outreach from Coach Andrew  

**Key Points:**
- Personal connection (Coach Andrew)
- Off-season training opportunity
- Practices at LBHS (familiar location)
- 16U & 18U divisions

---

### **Email 3: What is JTT? (Educational)**
**File:** `03-what-is-jtt-education.html`  
**Subject:** "What is Junior Team Tennis? Everything You Need to Know"  
**Use for:** Interested contacts who need more information  
**Goal:** Educate and overcome objections  

**Key Points:**
- Complete explanation of what JTT is
- What's included in the season
- What to expect (practices + matches)
- Is JTT right for your player?
- All divisions and pricing
- FAQ section
- Multiple CTAs

---

## 🎯 **Campaign Strategy**

### **3-Email Sequence**

**Week 1 - Tuesday:**
- Send Email 1 or 2 (depending on list)
- SMS follow-up 4 hours later

**Week 1 - Thursday:**
- Send Email 3 to contacts who opened Email 1/2 but didn't register
- This educates interested but hesitant parents

**Week 2 - Monday:**
- Final reminder email to unopened contacts
- Emphasize January 16th deadline

## 🚀 **How to Use**

### **For Ben (Campaign Manager)**

1. **Upload all 3 email templates** to GoHighLevel
2. **Create 3 separate campaigns** (one for each email)
3. **Schedule sends:**
   - Email 1/2: Tuesday 9am
   - Email 3: Thursday 10am (to engaged but not registered)
   - Reminder: Monday 9am (to unopened)
4. **Send SMS follow-ups** 4 hours after each email
5. **Monitor replies** and respond within 2 hours
6. **Track in Notion** JTT Interest Tracker
