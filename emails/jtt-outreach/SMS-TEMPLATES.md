## Response Templates

### If they reply "Interested" or "Tell me more"

```
Great! JTT is 15 weeks (Jan 12-Apr 26), 3x/week practices + weekend matches. Which division fits best - 16U or 18U? I can send you the full details.
```

### If they ask about pricing

```
Season is $2,800 (or $2,750 if paid upfront). Includes 3x/week practices, weekend matches, team shirt, and coach support. Payment plans available (2 or 4 installments, no fees). Worth chatting?
```

### If they ask about time commitment

```
Practices: Mon/Wed/Fri 6-8pm @ LBHS. Matches: Most Saturdays/Sundays Feb-Apr. Total ~90 hours over 15 weeks. Manageable with school schedule!
```

### If they're unsure about skill level

```
No worries! We have divisions for all levels (10U-18U). Happy to chat about which division would be the best fit for your player. When's a good time to call?
```

### If they say "Not right now"

```
No problem! If anything changes, we're here. Registration deadline is Jan 16th. Have a great spring! -Andrew
```

## Ben's SMS Workflow

### Step 1: Send Initial SMS (2-4 hours after email)
- Use template above
- Send via GoHighLevel SMS
- Track who opens/clicks

### Step 2: Monitor Replies
- Respond within 2 hours
- Use response templates above
- Move interested contacts to "JTT Hot Lead" tag

### Step 3: Follow-Up (if no reply after 24 hours)
**Don't send another SMS** - respect their space
**Instead:** Wait for email campaign Day 2 (if part of sequence)

### Step 4: Track in Notion
- Log all replies in Notion JTT Interest Tracker
- Note: Interested / Not Interested / No Response
- Schedule follow-up calls for "Interested" contacts

## SMS Best Practices

### ✅ Do:
- Keep under 160 characters (1 SMS)
- Include name personalization
- Provide clear next step (reply or call)
- Sign with name (Andrew or Ben)
- Include link to JTT page
- Send during business hours (9am-6pm)

### ❌ Don't:
- Send multiple SMS in same day
- Use ALL CAPS or excessive punctuation
- Send after 8pm or before 9am
- Include pricing in first SMS
- Be pushy or aggressive

## Contact Management

### Tag Structure in GoHighLevel

**After email sent:**
- Tag: "JTT Email Sent - Campaign 1" or "JTT Email Sent - Campaign 2"

**After SMS sent:**
- Tag: "JTT SMS Sent"

**After reply:**
- Tag: "JTT Interested" (if positive)
- Tag: "JTT Not Interested" (if negative)
- Tag: "JTT Follow-Up Needed" (if unclear)

**After registration:**
- Tag: "JTT Registered Spring 2026"
- Remove: All "JTT Interested" tags
