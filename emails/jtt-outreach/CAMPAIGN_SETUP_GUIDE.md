# JTT Spring 2026 Outreach Campaign Setup

Complete guide for Ben to set up both email + SMS campaigns in GoHighLevel.

---

## 📧 Campaign 1: General LBTA Families

### Email Campaign Setup

**Step 1: Upload Email Template**
1. Go to **Marketing → Templates → Email Templates**
2. Click **Create Template**
3. Name: "JTT Spring 2026 - General Families"
4. Paste HTML from `01-general-lbta-families.html`
5. Save template

**Step 2: Create Email Campaign**
1. Go to **Marketing → Campaigns → Email**
2. Click **Create Campaign**
3. Name: "JTT Spring 2026 - General LBTA Families"
4. Select template: "JTT Spring 2026 - General Families"
5. **Subject:** "Spring Junior Team Tennis — Spots Now Available"
6. **Preview text:** "15 weeks of team training and weekend matches for ages 10U-18U"
7. Select list: "All LBTA Contacts" or "LBTA Families"
8. Schedule: **Tuesday, 9:00 AM PST**
9. Save and schedule

**Step 3: Create SMS Follow-Up**
1. Go to **Marketing → Campaigns → SMS**
2. Click **Create Campaign**
3. Name: "JTT Spring 2026 - General Families SMS"
4. Message:
```
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, Andrew from LBTA here. Just sent you an email about Spring JTT (Junior Team Tennis). If interested, reply here or call Ben at (949) 464-6645. Details: lagunabeachtennisacademy.com/jtt
```
5. Select list: "All LBTA Contacts" (same as email)
6. Schedule: **Tuesday, 1:00 PM PST** (4 hours after email)
7. Save and schedule

## 🆘 Common Questions & Answers

### "What if my child hasn't played JTT before?"

> "No problem! We have players at all experience levels. The coaches assess everyone at the first practice and make sure they're in the right division. It's a great way to build match experience."

### "Can they join late?"

> "Team registration deadline is January 16th, but player registration is open until March 9th. The earlier they start, the more they benefit from the full 15 weeks of training."

### "What if they have a conflict with practice times?"

> "They need to attend at least 2 out of 3 practices per week. If that works, we can make it happen. Want to chat about their schedule?"

### "Do they need to be USTA registered?"

> "Yes, USTA registration is required for all JTT players. It's $33.15 and takes about 5 minutes online. We can send you instructions after you register."

### "What's the refund policy?"

> "If your player needs to withdraw before the season starts (Jan 12), full refund minus $100 admin fee. After season starts, prorated refund based on weeks remaining."
