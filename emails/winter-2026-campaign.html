TENNIS TIP

Win More Points With Your Feet

Most players think their strokes are the problem, but very often it's their footwork. Clean footwork puts you in balance, on time, and in position to swing freely.

1 Time your split-step to land exactly as your opponent hits the ball
2 Stay on your toes and use many small adjustment steps instead of big ones
3 Recover after contact by moving back toward your court position
