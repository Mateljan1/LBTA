The Three Mental Mistakes I See Every Day

Mistake #1: Playing the score, not the point

"I'm up 5-2, I just need to hold serve..."

And they tighten up. Double fault. Lose the game. Lose the set.

Fix: One point at a time. Literally nothing else matters.

Mistake #2: Trying too hard

"I NEED to hit a winner here!"

And they go for too much. Error. Lose the point.

Fix: Consistency beats brilliance. Make them beat you, don't beat yourself.

Mistake #3: Negative self-talk

"I'm playing terrible today."
"I can't hit my forehand."
"I always choke in the third set."

Your brain believes what you tell it.

Fix: Neutral language. "That one went long. Next one goes in."
