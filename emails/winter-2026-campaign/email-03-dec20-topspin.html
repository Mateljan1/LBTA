The Power Paradox

Here's what most players get wrong:

❌ They think: Power = Hitting harder

✅ Reality: Power = Hitting with spin + placement

Why spin matters more than speed:

1.  Topspin brings the ball down (prevents long balls)
2.  Spin creates margin (you can hit harder without going out)
3.  Spin bounces higher (pushes opponents back)
4.  Spin is controllable (speed without spin is chaos)

Federer doesn't hit harder than you. He hits with more spin at higher speeds. That's the difference.

 

How to Generate Topspin (Simple Version)

The Low-to-High Swing Path:

1.  Start low - Racquet head below the ball
2.  Brush up - Contact the back of the ball, move upward
3.  Finish high - Follow through over your opposite shoulder

Common mistakes:

❌ Swinging flat (no spin, no margin)
❌ Starting high (can't brush up if you're already up)
❌ Finishing low (follow-through creates spin)

The mental cue: "Wipe the windshield" - your swing path should curve from low to high like a windshield wiper.
