Today: The one drill every player should do (but nobody does).

The Wall Rally Drill

Find a wall. Any wall.

Stand 15 feet back.

Hit forehands against the wall. Try to rally with yourself.

GOAL: 50 consecutive hits

without missing

Why This Drill Is Magic

It reveals everything:

• Inconsistent contact point? Ball goes everywhere
• Poor footwork? You'll be reaching and off-balance
• No topspin? Ball flies over the wall
• Bad follow-through? No control

The wall doesn't lie.

It fixes everything:

• Forces you to focus on consistency (not power)
• Gives you instant feedback
• Builds muscle memory (50 reps = progress)
• Costs nothing (free practice anytime)

How to Progress the Drill

Week 1: 10 consecutive forehands

Week 2: 25 consecutive forehands

Week 3: 50 consecutive forehands

Week 4: Add backhands (alternate forehand/backhand)

Week 5: Add movement (side-to-side)

Every pro did this drill as a kid.

You should too.
