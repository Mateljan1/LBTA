What's Happening at LBTA This January

Happy New Year from all of us at Laguna Beach Tennis Academy.

As we head into 2026, I wanted to share a few updates and let you know what's happening on court.

Winter Quarter in Full Swing

Our Winter programs started January 5 and run through April 5. If you or your player haven't joined yet, there's still time — we prorate registration so you can jump in anytime.

Current programs accepting new players:

  • Junior Programs: Little Tennis Stars through High Performance
  • Adult Programs: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, LiveBall, and Cardio Tennis
  • Private Lessons: Available 7 days a week with flexible scheduling

JTT Spring Season Starts Monday

Our Junior Team Tennis season kicks off Monday, January 12. If your player is interested in competitive team experience with weekend matches, there are still spots available in most divisions (10U, 12U, 14U, 16U, 18U).

Reply to this email or text Ben at (949) 534-0457 for details.

Coaching Update

We're excited to announce that Coach Kevin will be leading all JTT sessions this season. Kevin brings consistency and expertise to our competitive programming, and families who've worked with him know what a difference that makes.

For Youth Development and High Performance, our coaching team continues to deliver structured, movement-first training at Alta Laguna and LBHS.

Looking Ahead: Ski Week Camp

Mark your calendars: Ski Week Camp runs February 10-14 during LBUSD Winter Break. It's a great way to keep players active and progressing while school is out.

Details coming soon, but reach out now if you want to reserve a spot early.
